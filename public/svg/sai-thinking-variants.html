SAI · thinking animation candidates

Existing variants for reference (top), new candidates below. All same logo, same color, same baseline tile size.

Existing — currently shipping

drift
Escalating clockwise spins (1×→2×→3×→4×) with mixed chevron timings, dog-shake settle. 28s loop.
drift-b · chase
Counter-clockwise spins, chevrons fire in tight left → top → right → bottom chase, short shimmy. 26s.
drift-c · salvo
Five quick clockwise spins, all four chevrons fire together at each beat, tight wobble settle. 22s.

New candidates

pulse · heartbeat
Chevrons breathe outward and back together while center contracts. No rotation. Steady 2.4s rhythm.
clockwork
Sharp 90° tick rotations with tiny overshoot, chevrons flash dim at each tick. Mechanical, second-hand feel. 8s.
scatter-gather
Chevrons explode outward, assembly spins 720° while exploded, then snaps back to center. 4.8s.
wave
Chevrons bob outward in a continuous clockwise wave: left → top → right → bottom. Hypnotic, no spin. 2.6s.
glitch
Hard stepped jolts with rotation, scale, and full blackouts. Larger displacements so it reads at small sizes. 2.4s.
inhale
Whole assembly squeezes inward while spinning, briefly overshoots on the way back out. Single deep breath. 5.4s.

More candidates · round 2

vortex
Chevrons spiral inward toward center while shrinking, center brightens at peak compression, spirals back out. 5s.
pendulum
Starts at center, swings to +60°, back through center, to -60°, returns. Chevrons whip slightly past on each swing. 3.6s.
comet
One chevron at a time fires far outward like a cannon and snaps back, cycling around the cardinals. 4.8s.
ripple
Center pulses then each chevron fade-pulses outward in sequence — a radial sonar wave from the core. 2.6s.
stutter
Tries to spin, gets stuck partway, jitters back, winds up again and finally breaks free into a full turn. 6.2s.

Round 3

flip
Whole logo flips like a card — rotates 360° while squashing vertically at each half-turn so it reads as a flip, not a spin. 4s.
typewriter
Each chevron briefly disappears and pops back in clockwise sequence — top, right, bottom, left — like the logo is being re-stamped. 3s.
morse
Chevrons stay mostly bright; brief "off" blips at different rhythms make the logo read as a signal lamp. 3.5s.
squish
Jelly: whole logo squashes horizontally, recoils, squashes vertically, recoils, settles. No rotation. 2.2s.
bloom
Closes into a tight bud while rotating, then spirals back open to full size with an overshoot bounce. 3.6s.
searchlight
Short scanning rotations back and forth (searching), then one big full sweep at the end. 3.4s.
